  Serena leads you down a long flight of stairs.
  
  You follow her through passageways that are so narrow you can barely squeeze 
through. Sometimes you can hear shouting nearby and see the flickering beams of
flashlights searching the dark corners where you were standing only seconds 
before. Finally, in a narrow alley, Serena stops and listens for a few moments.

  "See that donkey cart over there," she whispers. "It is from my village. I 
have arranged for it to meet me here. I will crawl into the bottom of it. Watch
how I do it, then follow."

  There is just room enough for you to squeeze into the cart. Serena arranges
the straw over the two of you and then signals to the driver with a low 
whistle.
